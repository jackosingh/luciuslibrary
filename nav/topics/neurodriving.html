Extra Corporal Mobility Device

"In Amerika kan je niet zonder, Faraday beschermt jou bij donder."

There is a transference of the sense of self when we get into the driver's seat of a car. There is this sublte intamacy, a grandiose trust. Our mind and body expand to novel boundries, those of the rims, front and rear bumper and our blind corners. Our vision of the road transmit itself to subtle adjustments of the (steering) wheel, which in turn, provides us feedback of our actions, further refining the steering.
